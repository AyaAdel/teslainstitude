COURSES AND FACULTY

Faculty

  • TOM SCHRACK, ARMRIT, CS - PROGRAM DIRECTOR

    Tom Schrack has been a leader in the MR field for 29 years. Tom'ss career includes advanced MR applications development for GE Healthcare, being the Manager of MR Education and Technical Development for Fairfax Radiological Consultants, and an Adjunct Instructor of MR Physics and Clinical Practices for Northern Virginia Community College. Tom has published numerous educational articles and modules for the International Center for Post Graduate Medical Education including Cardiac MR, Musculoskeletal MRI, and Technical Considerations of MR Imaging. Tom lectures annually to MRI technologists on a broad range of MR topics including Fetal MR, Cardiac, Prostate, 3.0 Tesla imaging, and Practical Uses of MRI Physics.

    Tom is credentialed by The American Registry of MRI Technologists and The Institute for Credentialing Excellence. He is a graduate of James Madison University and the Pittsburgh NMR Institute.

  • JIM MENGEL - ADVISOR

    Jim has more than 15 years with GE Healthcare holding positions as an MRI Sales Specialist and an Account Executive. In addition, Jim has had an important role in GE's renowned Commercial Leadership Program (CLP). In this role Jim has mentored many GE Leadership trainees.

    Jim also 20+ years of experience as a business owner in the fields in finance, management, sales, training and leadership roles.

    Jim is a graduate of the University of Maryland. Married with 3 adult children and 2 grandchildren. Jim's daughter, and son-in-law are all graduates of GE's CLP program.
